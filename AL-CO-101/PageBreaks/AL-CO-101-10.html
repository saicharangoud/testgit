Regional Care Collaborative Organization (RCCO)
A Regional Care Collaborative Organization (RCCO) connects Medicaid clients to Medicaid providers and helps clients find community and social services in their area. RCCOs help providers communicate with Medicaid clients and with each other, so Medicaid clients receive coordinated care. A RCCO will also help Medicaid clients get the right care when they are returning home from the hospital or a nursing facility, by providing the support needed for a quick recovery. Refer to the following link for information about RCCO service areas and contact numbers: www.colorado.gov/hcpf/regional-care-collaborative-organizations.
If the individual receives Medicaid and is enrolled in a RCCO, enter the name of the RCCO and the RCCO's phone number.
