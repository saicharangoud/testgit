This is to certify that this recipient is in need of an integrated set of assistance care services on a 24-hour
basis ,including at least two of the following four service components on the daily basis (check as
applicable):

Assistive with activities of daily living ,which is defined as individual assistance with ambulating,
transferring ,bathing, dressing ,eating ,grooming, and/or toileting

Assistive with instrumental activities of daily living ,which is defined as individual assistance
for personal items making telephone calls managing money,etc

Health support ,which is defined as observing the resident's whereabouts and well-being;
reminding the resident of any important tasks; and recording and reporting any significant
changes in appearance,behaviour, or state of health to the health care provider,designated
representative, or case manager.

Assistive with self-administration of medication, which is defined as assistance with or
supervision of self-administration of medication as permitted by law.
